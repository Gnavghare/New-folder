Upload Image for Body Analysis

How it works:

Upload a full-body image for our AI to analyze your body type, posture, symmetry, and proportions. We'll use this analysis to create a personalized fitness and nutrition plan.

Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG (max 16MB)
For best results:
  • Stand straight with arms slightly away from your body
  • Wear form-fitting clothing to allow accurate analysis
  • Ensure good lighting and a plain background
  • Make sure your full body is visible in the frame
What we'll analyze:
  • Body type and proportions
  • Posture quality and alignment
  • Body symmetry and balance
  • Estimated body fat percentage
  • BMI category
What you'll receive:
  • Detailed body analysis report
  • Personalized exercise recommendations
  • Posture correction exercises
  • Customized nutrition plan
  • Weekly workout schedule
